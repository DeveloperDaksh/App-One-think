Privacy Policy

Just One Thing is a mindful friction app designed to help you reduce impulsive app usage.

Data Collection

We believe in Privacy First. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or app usage statistics to external servers. All data, including which apps you block and your success/failure rates, are stored locally on your device.

Permissions

This app requires "Usage Access" to detect when you open a blocked app. This data is processed in real-time on your device and is never shared.
